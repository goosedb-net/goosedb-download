GooseDB

A feature-extended fork of DuckDB with server/client, multi-session, and concurrent-write support using the PostgreSQL wire protocol.

Why GooseDB?

GooseDB takes the simplicity and speed of DuckDB and extends it for more robust, enterprise-grade use cases. With server/client capabilities, multi-session handling, and concurrent-write operations, GooseDB is suitable for both small-scale analytics and large-scale production environments.

Key Features

  • Server/Client Support: Easily deploy GooseDB in client-server architectures for greater flexibility.
  • Multi-Session Handling: Manage multiple concurrent sessions without performance bottlenecks.
  • Concurrent Writes: Update and insert data even while queries are running.
  • PostgreSQL Wire Protocol: Connect seamlessly with common PostgreSQL-compatible tools and libraries.
  • DuckDB Foundation: Built on top of DuckDB, retaining its in-memory analytics performance.
